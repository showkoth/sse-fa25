\section*{Q1. Purpose of \{\% csrf\_token \%\}}
The \{\% csrf\_token \%\} tag inserts a hidden form field containing a CSRF token, 
which Djangoâ€™s middleware checks against the CSRF cookie. This ensures that 
form submissions originate from trusted sources and protects against Cross-Site

\section*{Q2. Consequences of forgetting \{\% csrf\_token \%\}}
If the tag is omitted:
\begin{itemize}[noitemsep]
    \item Django rejects the request with a \textbf{403 Forbidden} error since the CSRF check fails.
    \item The application becomes vulnerable to CSRF attacks, where a malicious site could perform unauthorized actions.
    \item Legitimate users may be blocked from submitting valid forms.
\end{itemize}
